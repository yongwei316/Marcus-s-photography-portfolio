ABOUT ME

MARCUS BEH

I'm a self-taught photographer based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Among the wide variety of genres in photography, I personally enjoy taking landscape / urban photos the most. The journey of exploring unknown places and looking at views from different perspectives is somehow thrilling and exciting for me. I strive to improve my skills and explore all the aspects of photography with the aspiration of perfecting my craft.
